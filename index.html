Welcome to VFC

Virtual Farming Community

Virtual Farming community, is a community of virtual farmers that come together under the same ideals. Those include a well rounded community of people, a group of people that play under the same realism that they do.

Question:

How do I start?

Answer

Click the Registration button above to fill out the Registration application. Then you are going to join the discord go to the rules chat and type "-agree". A admin or dev member will be with you shortly to complete the registration.

Question:

I signed up, now what?

Answer

A member of the VFC staff will contact you shortly. They will accept your account and you will be able to log in. If your request is not accept with the first week feel free to dm one of the staff. Once you are accepted there will be more guidelines to look at on how you should conduct your farm and or business.

Question:

What are the time

commitments?

Answer

It really depends. If you want you could get on everyday. But getting on once every season or during key events like harvest and planting will most likely suffice. And the time it takes to harvest or plant also depends on type of equipment and other factors. ie, how big the field is.

Question:

How does Seasons work?

Answer

In VFC we run 3 day season which means that one year in VFC is 12 days. The servers are set a 1x speed and crops grow about once a day.
